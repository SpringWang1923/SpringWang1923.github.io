职场英语复习题

职场英语复习题

复习内容

1.1-7单元文章（除去第2单元文章）40分（2课内2课外）。

2.课后判断题（10题，10分）。

3.课后匹配题（10题，10分）。

4.作文，跟课文相关（40分）。

相关说明

因为时间过于仓促，难免有地方疏漏或错误。P120第七单元的短文对话排序并未整理，中文翻译均为机翻，存在翻译错误的地方，还望谅解，仅仅是临时应付考试用的。预祝各位取得好成绩！

A

课后判断题P13

\1. Dress conservatively and professionally. T

穿着保守而专业

2. Turn up 15 minutes earlier for the interview. F

提前15分钟来面试

3. Shake hands with the interviewers while looking them in the eye. T

看着面试官的眼睛，与他们握手。

\4. Treat the interviewers with all you respect, neglect others, like receptionist etc. F

尊重面试官忽略其他人，如接待员等。

5. Keep your back straight and sit bolt upright in your chair. F

保持背部挺直，笔直地坐在椅子上。

\6. Talk to the interviewers as if they were your friends. F

与面试官交谈，就好像他们是你的朋友一样。

7. Don’t be the icebreaker. T

不要成为破冰者。

TEXT A

面试礼仪，职业装，时间上提前到， 肢体语言，言谈，事后礼仪

B

课后判断题P18

\1. The complete lack of preparation is the number one problem with job candidates. T

完全缺乏准备是求职者的首要问题。

\2. If you apply for a job of accountant in the hospital, you don’t need to know anything about the health care industry. F

如果你在医院申请会计工作，你不需要了解任何有关医疗行业的知识。

\3. You should learn more about the jobs you’re interested in. T

你应该多了解你感兴趣的工作。

\4. If candidates have substantial education, training, and work experience, they can do a good job of presenting their skills for that job in the interview. F

如果应聘者具有自己丰富的教育，培训，和工作经验，他们可以在面试中很好地展示自己的技能。

5. If you read the job description before the interview and mention the key skills that job requires in an interview, your chances of being employed will be increased. T

如果你在面试前阅读了工作描述，并在面试中提到了工作所需的关键技能，你被录用的机会就会增加。

\6. Knowing the specific skills a job requires can help you make a better resume because you can focus on those skills in your resume. T

了解一份工作需要的具体技能可以帮助你制作一份更好的简历，因为你可以在简历中专注于这些技能。

\7. The school career center can provide some information of the company usually. T

学校就业中心通常可以提供公司的一些信息。

8. To conduct company research, you can’t ignore the Web. T

要进行公司调查，你不能忽视网络。

9. You should know the company through the company’s advertisement. T

你可以通过公司的广告了解公司。

10. It is unnecessary to get in touch with the alumni who work for the company. F

没有必要与为公司工作的校友联系。

TEXT B

课后判断题P33

\1. A board of directors has a different name, such as board of trustees, board of governors, board of managers, or executive board. T

董事会具有不同名称,如董事会，经理委员会，或执行委员会。

\2. Typically the board chooses one of its members to be the chair or chairperson of the board of directors, traditionally also called CEO. F

通常，董事会会选择其一名成员担任董事会主席或董事会主任，传统上也称为首席执行官。

\3. The chairman is the highest officer of an organized group such as a board, committee, or deliberative assembly. T

主席是有组织团体的最高官员。

\4. The chairman is the head of the management committee and usually reports to the board, which is headed by the CEO. F

董事会是管理委员会的负责人，通常向由首席执行官领导的董事会报告。

5. In the case of companies and similarly-organized bodies, there are generally two types of Chair: Non-executive and Executive. T

就公司和类似组织机构而言，通常有俩种类型的主席；非执行主席，和执行主席。

\6. A CEO usually has several subordinate executives such as a chief financial officer(CFO), chief operating officer (COO), chief technical officer (CTO), chief marketing officer (CMO),chief information officer(CIO), chief creative officer(CCO). T

首席执行官通常有几个下属执行官，如首席财务官，首席运营官，首席技术官，首席营销官，首席信息官，首席创意官。

\7. The board of directors manages day to day contact—writes press releases etc. F

董事会负责管理日常联系人，撰写新闻稿等。

课后判断题P52

  1. It doesn’t matter that you leave the phone ringing for a long time without picking it up. F

长时间不接电话铃声并不重要。

  1. It is wrong to identify yourself when you are answering the phone. F

接听电话时表明自己的身份是错误的

  1. You’ll need to understand how to transfer calls internally and you should also keep a pen and pad handy so you can jot down details of the call. T

您需要了解如何在内部转接电话，还应该随身携带笔和垫，以便记下通话的详细信息。

  1. The information you could be looking to gather might include the caller’s name, company name (if applicable), time and date of call, reason for call and their contact details. T

您可能希望收集的信息可能包括呼叫者的姓名，公司名称（如果适用），呼叫的时间和日期，呼叫原因及其联系方式

  1. It is a good practice to let the caller hang up before you do. T

最好先让呼叫者挂断电话。

  1. It doesn’t matter that you answer the phone while eating. F

吃饭时接电话并不重要。

  1. You could always answer the personal call at work and talk for a long time. F

您始终可以在工作中接听个人电话并长时间交谈

课后判断题P56

\1. You should identify yourself and your company first when you make a business phone call. T

当您拨打商务电话时，您应该首先表明自己和公司的身份

\2. When you are connected with the person, state the purpose of your calland then be sure to ask if you are calling at a convenient time. T

当您与该人联系时，请说明呼叫的目的，然后请务必询问您是否在方便的时间拨打电话。

\3. When you lesve a message or voicemail for someone, make it as long as possible, leave the details there to make sure others are clear about your message. F

当您为某人发送消息或语音邮件时，请尽可能长时间地保留详细信息，以确保其他人清楚您的消息

\4. Speakerphones are useful for conference calls. If you are leading a speakerphone meeting with a number of people, allow each person to introduce himself or herself, to help the listeners match a name to a voice. T

免提电话对于电话会议非常有用。如果您正在与多人主持免提电话会议，请允许每个人介绍自己，以帮助听众将姓名与声音相匹配。

\5. People can’t move around in the room when using a special hands-free headset-type telephone. F

使用特殊的免提耳机型电话时，人们无法在房间内走动

课后判断题P70

\1. As a chairperson, before holding a meeting, you should also decide who should attend and what the purpose of the meeting is. T

作为主席，在举行会议之前，您还应该决定谁应该参加以及会议的目的是什么。

\2. It is better to distribute the meeting agenda three days prior to the meeting day. T

最好在会议日前三天分发会议议程。

\3. It is OK that you begin a meeting five to seven minutes after it was scheduled. F

您可以在预定会议后五到七分钟开始会议。

\4. It is not necessary to the agenda during the meeting. F

会议期间不需要议程。

\5. We should respect different viewpoints during the meeting. T

在会议期间，我们应该尊重不同的观点。

\6. A well-planned agenda, posted ground rules, and using a bin can help meeting run effectively. T

精心策划的议程、张贴的基本规则和使用垃圾箱可以帮助会议有效运行。

\7. The relaxing atmosphere is not good for the meeting to be productive. F

轻松的气氛不利于会议的成效

课后判断题P89

\1. Collect information in file folder. Make it the repository for contact names, airline tickets, hotel and so on. T

收集文件夹中的信息。使其成为联系人姓名，机票，酒店等的存储库。

\2. Dress appropriately for maximum comfort, if you are not traveling with a client. T

如果您不与客户一起旅行，请穿着得体以获得最大的舒适度

\3. Eat whatever you like on the plane. F

在飞机上吃任何你喜欢的东西。

\4. Try to give yourself a few hours rest when you get to your hotel or accommodation, if you are not hurry for the meeting. T

当你到达酒店或住宿时，试着给自己几个小时的休息时间，如果你不赶会议的话。

\5. For the informal activities, man could dress casually such as sport jackets, slacks, and light weight sweaters. T

对于非正式活动，男士可以随意穿着，例如运动夹克，休闲裤和轻便的毛衣

\6. For women, conservatism is again the key requirement, No backless, sleeveless, or strapless dresses for business. T

对于女性来说，保守主义再次成为关键要求，商务上不能穿露背、无袖或无肩带连衣裙

\7. It doesn’t matter if you get late for the meeting for 2 or 3 minutes. F

如果您迟到2或3分钟并不重要。

课后判断题P94

\1. Business lunch or dinner usually means you should wear a suit or other professional attire. T

商务午餐或晚餐通常意味着您应该穿西装或其他职业装

\2. Greet your host. Hugging is the usual way, particularly if it is a business function. F

问候你的主人。拥抱是通常的方式，特别是如果它是一种业务功能。

\3. It is impolite to sit down before your host say to sit, no matter they are seated or not. F

在主人说要坐之前坐下来是不礼貌的，无论他们是否就座

\4. Put your napkin on your chest, F

将餐巾纸放在胸前

\5. White wine is recommended for res meat and heavy dishes; res for fish, chicken. And vegetables. F

白葡萄酒推荐用于肉类和重度菜肴;鱼，鸡肉。还有蔬菜。

\6. For the formal sinner setting, knives are on the left and forks are on the right. F

对于正式的晚餐设置，刀在左边，叉子在右边。

\7. If someone asks for the salt, pass salt only. F

如果有人要求盐，只传递盐。

\8. When butter is being passed, cut a pat and place it on your bread plate. T

当黄油被传递，切一拍，把它放在你的面包盘子上。

\9. Don’t put your elbows on the table. T

不要把你的胳膊肘放在桌子上

10.Use the restroom to pick food out of your teeth or repair your makeup. T

你可以在洗手间里从牙齿里挑出食物或修复化妆品

匹配题P105

\1. George, how about having dinner with us tonight?

Oh, how nice of you! I’ll be very glad to come.

乔治，今晚和我们一起吃晚饭怎么样？

啊，你真好！我会很高兴来的

\2. I’ll meet you at the entrance of the hotel at six tomorrow evening. Is that OK?

Very well. I’ll be waiting for you then.

我明天晚上六点在酒店门口见你。可以吗？

很好。那我就等你了

\3. May I propose a toast to brisk business and continuing development?

Thanks. Here’s to your health and success in business as well.

我是否可以为业务的蓬勃发展和持续发展干杯？

谢谢。这也是为了您的健康和在商业上的成功。

\4. May I have the bill, please?

Certainly, here you are.

请问我可以拿到账单吗？

当然，你在这里

\5. Waitress, can I pay with a traveler’s check?

I’m afraid not. We can only accept cash.

女服务员，我可以用旅行支票付款吗？

恐怕不是。我们只接受现金。

\6. Miss, what’s this amount for?

It’s the service charge.

Miss，这个金额是干什么用的？

这是服务费。

\7. Sir, may I take a print of your card?

Just a moment. I’ll get a print of your card?

Sir，我可以打印你的卡吗？

只是一会儿。我会得到你的卡的打印件？

\8. Please put it on my bill.

Certainly sir. Thank you.

请把它放在我的账单上。

当然是先生。谢谢。

课后判断题P112

\1. You’d better make sure guest is aware of the purpose of the gathering at the initial invitation. T

最好确保客人在最初的邀请中了解聚会的目的

\2. A vegetarian dish in the menu is necessary. T

菜单上的素食菜肴是必要的。

\3. It is the duty of the guest to “call the meal to order.” F

我是客人的职责，”叫餐点餐”。

\4. All good guests at a business or social meal will wait for the host to begin before starting their own meals. As a host, don’t keep them waiting. T

所有参加商务或社交活动的客人都会等待主人开始自己的用餐。作为主持人，不要让他们等待。

\5. You’d better call other’s attention to the fact that you are paying the bill. F

你最好提请别人注意你正在支付账单的事实。

课后判断题P125

\1. Exhibitors usually sell more products at trade shows than sales calls. F

参展商通常在贸易展览会上销售的产品多于销售电话

\2. It takes a comparatively short amount of time to reach the customers at trade show. T

在贸易展上接触客户需要相对较短的时间。

\3. Everyone who visits your display knows the detail of your product or service. F

访问您的展示广告的每个人都知道您的产品或服务的详细信息

\4. The most valuable thing a trade show provides is opportunity for future communications. T

贸易展提供的最有价值的东西是未来沟通的机会。

在派遣展位工作人员之前，您需要考虑举办贸易展的地方的天气。
